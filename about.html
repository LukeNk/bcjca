BCJCA is about innovation leadership teamwork network

The British Columbia Junior Chess Association is a chess organization directed towards bringing together secondary school students to play chess. Through tournaments, events, and casual play, its main goal is to not only play chess together, but to create life long connections among British Columbian youth and share our passion for the game. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, BCJCA is the place for you!
